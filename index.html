🛒 Vak Store Trading Calculator

What this service is

We organize and facilitate trade among players in Vintage Story on The Official Public Server (TOPS). We run a main store for instant trades, track pricing data across traded items, and provide a player-to-player trading platform (OCM).

This site is a player-driven in-game trading hub and calculator for the Vintage Story TOPS server — no real‑world goods or money are exchanged; all prices refer to in‑game items only.

Main store (instant trades): buy/sell common items quickly at listed prices.
Price tracking: we record historical trade pricing to improve transparency and help with fair trading.
OCM marketplace: players can list and trade directly with each other, using the same pricing and tooling.

About the currency (EW)(Eange Weight):
EW is designed to be a fully liquid trading currency. Its value is not permanently pegged to one specific metal or item. The goal is to make trading smoother by giving players a common "trade-anything" medium: you can earn EW by selling items and spend EW to buy many other items the store offers.

Backing / insurance (when funded):
When the system has sufficient reserves, we back EW with metals. If you hold more than 500 EW, you can request the equivalent value in metals to be delivered to your deposit box as an always-accessible "insurance" option. The long-term goal is to maintain more value in metal reserves than EW in circulation, so even a "bank run" would not collapse the system (this was achieved last wipe; 2026 wipe will depend on how quickly reserves build up again).
