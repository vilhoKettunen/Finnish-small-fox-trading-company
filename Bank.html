EW Bank Dashboard

This dashboard provides a transparent view of the EW currency ecosystem — how much EW is in circulation, how it is issued and destroyed, how the store's metal reserves back the currency, and how wealth is distributed among players.

All data is updated once per day at midnight. No login required.
