?? EW Insurance — Policy Manager

EW Insurance lets you lock in the current sell-price value of your EW as physical metals. You deposit EW in units of 500 EW. Your chosen % allocation across metals determines which ingots and nuggets are reserved for you. Admin approves every change. On approval, your EW balance is debited (deposit) or credited (withdrawal) automatically.

  • Each unit = 500 EW. No fractions.
  • All metal prices use the current SELL price at time of admin approval.
  • Steel is ingot-only; any EW remainder after steel ingots is shown as "Uninsured EW".
  • Up to 5 policies per account.
