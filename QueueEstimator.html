Queue Time Estimator

How to Use

Upload or paste your Vintage Story game log file to estimate how long it will take to reach queue position 0. The estimator analyzes your queue progression and calculates an average time based on your historical data.

Note: Estimates are based on the data in your log file and may vary based on current server load and other factors. For best accuracy, use a log file covering at least 30 minutes of queue time.

Finding Your Log File

Windows

Navigate to your Vintage Story logs folder:

%appdata%\VintagestoryData\Logs\

Quick steps:

  1. Press Win + R to open the Run dialog
  2. Type: %appdata%\VintagestoryData\Logs
  3. Press Enter
  4. Look for client-main.log

Linux

Navigate to your Vintage Story logs folder:

~/.config/VintagestoryData/Logs/

Quick steps:

  1. Open your file manager
  2. Press Ctrl + H to show hidden files
  3. Navigate to: .config → VintagestoryData → Logs
  4. Look for client-main.log

Or use terminal:

cat ~/.config/VintagestoryData/Logs/client-main.log

⬆️ Uploading Your Log

Once you've found client-main.log:

  1. Drag & drop: Drag the client-main.log file directly onto the upload area below
  2. Or click: Click the upload area to browse and select the file
  3. Or copy: Open the log file with a text editor, select all (Ctrl+A), copy, and paste the content into the textarea below
