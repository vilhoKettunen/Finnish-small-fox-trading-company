How to Use

Upload or paste your Vintage Story game log file to estimate how long it will take to reach queue position 0. The estimator analyzes your queue progression and calculates an average time based on your historical data.

Note: Estimates are based on the data in your log file and may vary based on current server load and other factors. For best accuracy, use a log file covering at least 30 minutes of queue time.
