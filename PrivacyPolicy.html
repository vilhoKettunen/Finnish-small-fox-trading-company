2. What Data We Collect

  • Account information (Google Login): email address and basic profile fields provided by Google Sign-In (we do not collect or store passwords).
  • Profile information you provide: username (player name) and mailbox fields (including optional mall mailbox).
  • Trading / transaction / history data: records needed to operate the tool and preserve transparency/integrity of trading history. These records are linked to an internal user ID and may include your chosen username.
  • Leaderboards preference: whether you opt in/out of public leaderboards (OUT / HALF / FULL), including an internal anonymous label/ID used for HALF opt-in.
  • Statistics cache: computed summaries of your activity used to display your personal statistics and, if opted in, leaderboards.
  • Technical data / local device storage: we may store a Google login token in your browser (localStorage and/or cookies) to restore your session. You can remove it by logging out and/or clearing your browser storage.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • To provide login and account functionality.
  • To operate and maintain the service (including account settings, trading tools, and history views).
  • To provide transparency and integrity of trading history, and to compute statistics.
  • To improve the functionality and safety of the tool.
We do not use your email for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Article6)

  • Performance of a contract (Art.6(1)(b)): processing needed to provide access and core service features.
  • Legitimate interests (Art.6(1)(f)): operating, securing, and improving the service; preserving transaction integrity and transparency.
  • Consent (Art.6(1)(a)): public leaderboards participation is opt-in and can be changed in Account Settings.

5. Data Retention

  • Your email is stored while your account remains active.
  • If you delete your account, your email is removed from the system.
  • Important: historical trading/transaction/history data may be retained for transparency and integrity purposes.
  • After account deletion, retained historical data is generally linked to an internal user ID and/or historical username snapshots. This means it is pseudonymized, not fully anonymized.

6. Data Sharing / Third-Party Services

  • We do not sell or rent personal data.
  • We use third-party providers to operate the service. In particular, Google and Cloudflare may process data as part of providing authentication and hosting/delivery services.
  • For more details, please refer to the providers’ privacy policies.
